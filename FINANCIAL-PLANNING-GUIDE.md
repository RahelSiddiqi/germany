**When You DON'T Need Blocked Account:**

-   ‚úÖ You have DAAD or other full scholarship (scholarship letter replaces it)
-   ‚úÖ German family/sponsor provides affidavit (Verpflichtungserkl√§rung)
-   ‚úÖ Some embassies accept other proof of funds (check YOUR country's German embassy)

**How to Open Blocked Account:**

**Option 1: Expatrio (Most Popular, Fully Online)**

-   Cost: ‚Ç¨11,904 deposit + ‚Ç¨49 opening fee

-   Cost: ‚Ç¨11,904 deposit + ‚Ç¨89 opening fee
