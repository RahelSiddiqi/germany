## üìö Targeted Review: Speaking

### Fluency Improvement

**If many pauses:**

-   Practice thinking aloud
-   Use fillers appropriately: "Well, that's an interesting question..."
-   Record and re-record

**If too fast/slow:**

-   Practice with metronome
-   Record and listen
-   Match native speakers

### Vocabulary Improvement

**If repetitive:**

-   Learn synonyms for common words
-   Practice collocations
-   Use topic-specific vocabulary

**If simple:**

-   Learn idiomatic expressions
-   Practice phrasal verbs
-   Use advanced vocabulary

### Pronunciation Improvement

**If unclear:**

-   Focus on word stress
-   Practice sentence intonation
-   Record and compare
