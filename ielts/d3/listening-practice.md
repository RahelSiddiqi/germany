## âš ï¸ Common Listening Traps

### Trap 1: Changed Information

> "The meeting is on Tuesday... sorry, I meant Wednesday."

-   **Answer:** Wednesday (not Tuesday!)

### Trap 2: Distractor Options

> "You could take the bus or train. The train is more convenient."

-   **Answer:** Train (preferred option)

### Trap 3: Negative Structures

> "We decided NOT to include the chapter on statistics."

-   **Answer:** Statistics is excluded

### Trap 4: Conditionals

> "If we had more time, we'd include interviews."

-   **Answer:** Interviews are NOT included (hypothetical)

### Trap 5: Multiple Numbers

> "The course was Â£250 last year but now it's Â£280."

-   **Answer:** Â£280 (current price)
