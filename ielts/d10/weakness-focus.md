### Writing Task 2 Intensive

**Quick Structure Check:**

**Introduction (2 sentences):**

1. Paraphrase topic
2. State your position/outline

**Body 1 (5-6 sentences):**

1. Topic sentence (main point)
2. Explanation
3. Example
4. Result/analysis
5. Link to thesis

**Body 2 (5-6 sentences):**

1. Topic sentence (second point)
2. Explanation
3. Example
4. Result/analysis
5. Link to thesis

**Conclusion (2-3 sentences):**

1. Restate position
2. Summarize key points
3. Final thought/recommendation
