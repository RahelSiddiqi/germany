## **ğŸš€ FINAL TIPS FOR 8.5 BAND SCORE**

### Listening 8.5 Secret:

-   Predict answers **before** speaker talks
-   Focus on connectors ("because," "therefore")
-   Practice **specific accent variations** (British, Australian, American)

### Reading 8.5 Secret:

-   Master **paraphrasing** (synonyms, grammatical transformation)
-   Spot keywords **instantly** (skim in 30 sec max)
-   T/F/Not Given: distinguish "opposite" vs "not mentioned"

### Writing 8.5 Secret:

-   Task 1: Describe **trends**, not just numbers
-   Task 2: Use **cohesive devices** (furthermore, in addition, conversely)
-   Aim for **complexity** in sentence structures (passive, conditionals)

### Speaking 8.5 Secret:

-   **Fluency**: Speak naturally, don't memorize
-   **Coherence**: Link ideas with connectors
-   **Vocabulary**: Use **subject-specific** terms naturally
-   **Pronunciation**: Clarity > perfect accent
